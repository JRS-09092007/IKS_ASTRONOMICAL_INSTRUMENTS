DIGAMSA YANTRA

Jantar Mantar • Jaipur • Est. 1734

An advanced azimuth-measuring instrument. A string is stretched from the central pillar, aligned with the Sun (or a star), and read against the outer circular marble scale to find its exact compass direction.

Visual Guide: The bright yellow line represents the observer's string pointing towards the Sun, marking the Azimuth angle on the scale.
